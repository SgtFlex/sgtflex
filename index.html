Hi, I'm Aaron Ramos. Ever since I was young, I was always fascinated with video games as a medium for art. I believed they were the perfect combination of all the different things: Music, Art, and Programming. I grew up playing and experiencing tons of different video games. As I went into college, I decided to major in Computer Science as I loved programming so much, and I took up 3D Art and Game Production as an aside in my own personal time. I was, and continue to be passionate about all of the above, and I am always striving to and capable of learning new things, as I know the only restraint is time.
